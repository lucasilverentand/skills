# Images
**Avoid mirroring images, including photographs, illustrations, and general artwork.** Flipping an image often changes its intended meaning; furthermore, flipping a copyrighted visual could lead to infringement. If the content of an image is strongly dependent on reading direction, consider generating a replacement version rather than mirroring the original. **Adjust the placement of images when their sequence is important.** For instance, if multiple visuals are presented in a specific arrangement—such as chronological, alphabetical, or based on user preference—reverse their positions to maintain the intended sequence in an RTL context.
