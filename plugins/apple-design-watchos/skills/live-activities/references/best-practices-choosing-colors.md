# Choosing colors
- **Carefully consider using a custom background color and opacity.** Background customization is unavailable for compact, minimal, or expanded presentations. Nevertheless, you may apply a custom background color to the Lock Screen presentation. If you define a custom background image or color for the Lock Screen, ensure adequate contrast, particularly when using tint colors on devices equipped with an Always-On display operating at reduced luminance.
- **Use color to express the character and identity of your app.** Live Activities displayed in the Dynamic Island utilize a solid black, opaque background. You should consider employing vibrant colors for text and objects to communicate your app's brand and personality. Bold hues ensure the Live Activity is immediately identifiable, differentiates itself from other activities, and feels like a small, glanceable extension of your application. Furthermore, bold colors assist in reinforcing the relationships between elements within the Live Activity itself.
- **Tint your Live Activity’s key line color so that it matches your content.** When the background is dark—such as in Dark Mode—a key line appears around the Dynamic Island to differentiate it from surrounding content. Select a key line color that aligns with the colors used by other components in your Live Activity. For developer guidance, refer to [Creating custom views for Live Activities](apple:ActivityKit/creating-custom-views-for-live-activities#Use-custom-colors).
