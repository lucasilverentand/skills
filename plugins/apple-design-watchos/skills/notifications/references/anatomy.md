# Anatomy
Notifications may utilize different styles depending on the platform, including:

- A banner or view displayed on a Lock Screen, Home Screen, Home View, or desktop
- An app icon badge count
- An entry within Notification Center

Furthermore, notifications concerning direct communication (such as a message or phone call) can employ an interface distinct from non-communication alerts. These direct communication notifications should feature prominent contact images (or *avatars*) and group names rather than relying solely on the application icon.
