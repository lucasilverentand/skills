# Auto-renewable subscriptions — overview
- **Highlight subscription benefits during the onboarding process.** By demonstrating the value of your service upon initial app launch, you can educate users on its functionality and clarify what they gain by subscribing. Include a clear call to action and a concise summary of subscription terms (referencing [Making signup effortless](#Making-signup-effortless)). For additional guidance, consult [Onboarding](onboarding.md).
- **Provide a variety of content options, service tiers, and durations.** Users value the ability to select the subscription plan that best matches their requirements.
- **Evaluate offering users complimentary access before requiring payment.** Limited free usage allows individuals to sample your content and motivates those who have already engaged with the material to commit. Examples include implementing a freemium model, a usage-based paywall, or a free trial.
- **Prompt subscriptions at opportune moments, such as when they approach the cap on their complimentary content.** Furthermore, consider placing prompts throughout your application to allow users to subscribe at any time.
- **Target new subscriptions only when the user is not currently subscribed.** Otherwise, users might mistakenly believe their current subscription has expired. If you offer identical subscription options across multiple applications or your website, ensure a sign-in feature is available so users do not feel compelled to pay again for the same service.
