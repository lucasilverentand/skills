# Helping people manage their subscriptions
Providing subscription management allows users to upgrade, downgrade, or cancel a service without exiting your application. Integrating subscription handling within your app also provides a natural setting to address common subscriber issues and present alternative offers.

- **Provide summaries of the customer’s subscriptions.** Specifically, users value seeing their upcoming renewal date without needing to search for it. Consider displaying this information in a dedicated account or settings screen, near the subscription management option. For developer guidance, refer to [Product.SubscriptionInfo](apple:StoreKit/Product/SubscriptionInfo).
- **Consider using the system-provided subscription-management UI.** By utilizing StoreKit APIs, you can offer a unified experience that enables users to manage or cancel their subscriptions without leaving your app. For developer guidance, see [showManageSubscriptions(in:)](apple:StoreKit/AppStore/showManageSubscriptions(in:)).
- **Consider ways to encourage a subscriber to keep their subscription or resubscribe later.** When you use StoreKit APIs, your app receives notification when a user cancels their subscription. In this situation, you might wish to extend a tailored offer as an alternative to cancellation or invite users to provide feedback on their cancellation reasons in an exit survey. Beyond offering insights into customer challenges, survey responses can also inform your retention and win-back messaging strategies.
- **Always make it easy for customers to cancel an auto-renewable subscription.** If the option to manage subscriptions is buried deep within your app or difficult to locate, subscribers may feel discouraged or prevented from canceling.
- **Consider creating a branded, contextual experience to complement the system-provided management UI.** Within your custom interface, you could feature a popular premium tier or offer personalized suggestions for alternative plans based on the customer's usage patterns or preferences. For instance, you could introduce a promotional offer providing a discounted rate for a limited duration. You might also consider subscription [offer codes](#Supporting-offer-codes) to assist in winning back lapsed subscribers and encouraging upgrades from current users.
