# Platform guidance — macOS
- **Do not place controls or crucial information at the bottom of a window.** Users often shift windows so that the lower edge extends below the screen boundary.
- **Do not display content within the camera housing at the top edge of the window.** For guidance intended for developers, consult [NSPrefersDisplaySafeAreaCompatibilityMode](apple:BundleResources/Information-Property-List/NSPrefersDisplaySafeAreaCompatibilityMode).
