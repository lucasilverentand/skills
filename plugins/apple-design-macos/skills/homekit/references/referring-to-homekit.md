# Referring to HomeKit
Your application must be the primary focus, minimizing the prominence of any references to HomeKit or Apple Home.

**Compliance with Apple's trademark guidelines is mandatory.** Apple trademarks must not appear in your application name or visuals. When using product names within text, they must match the exact usage specified on the [Apple Trademark List](https://www.apple.com/legal/intellectual-property/trademark/appletmlist.html).

*   Use singular forms for all Apple product names; do not use possessive forms.
*   Do not translate "Apple," "Apple Home," "HomeKit," or any other Apple trademark.
*   Avoid generic category descriptors (e.g., use "iPad," not "tablet").
*   Do not imply any form of sponsorship, partnership, or endorsement from Apple.
*   Ensure proper attribution and credit lines for Apple, HomeKit, and all other Apple trademarks where legal information is displayed in the app.
*   Mention Apple devices and operating systems exclusively within technical specifications or compatibility details.

||Example text|
|---|---|
|✓|Use HomeKit to turn on your lights from your iPhone or iPad.|
|✗|Use HomeKit to turn on your lights from your iOS devices.|

Refer to [Guidelines for Using Apple Trademarks](https://www.apple.com/legal/intellectual-property/guidelinesfor3rdparties.html).

#### Referencing HomeKit and the Home app
**Ensure proper capitalization when referencing *HomeKit*.** *HomeKit* is a single term, capitalized as *H* and *K*, followed by lowercase letters. *Apple Home* is two words, capitalized as *A* and *H*, followed by lowercase letters. If your layout only uses all-uppercase designations, you may typeset *HomeKit* or *Apple Home* in all caps to match the surrounding style.

**Do not use *HomeKit* as a descriptive noun.** Instead, employ phrases such as *works with*, *use*, *supports*, or *compatible*.

||Example text|
|---|---|
|✓|[Brand] lightbulbs work with HomeKit.|
|✓|HomeKit-enabled thermostat.|
|✓|You can use HomeKit with [App Name].|
|✗|HomeKit lightbulbs.|

**Do not imply that HomeKit is performing an action or function.**

||Example text|
|---|---|
|✓|Back door is unlocked with HomeKit.|
|✗|HomeKit unlocked the back door.|

**You may include the name *Apple* alongside *HomeKit*, if appropriate.**

||Example text|
|---|---|
|✓|Compatible with Apple HomeKit.|

**Use the term *HomeKit* when providing setup, configuration, or instructional details, if necessary.**

||Example text|
|---|---|
|✓|Open HomeKit settings.|

**Always use the app name *Apple Home* when specifically referring to the application. On its initial mention in the body copy, use the full name *Apple Home*. Subsequent references may refer to the Home app.**

||Example text|
|---|---|
|✓|Open the Apple Home app.|
|✓|Open the Apple Home app. Your accessory and room will now appear in the Home app.|
|✗|Open Home.|
