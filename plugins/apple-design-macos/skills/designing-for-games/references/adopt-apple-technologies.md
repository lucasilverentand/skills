# Adopt Apple technologies
- **Integrate Game Center to enable players to find your game across devices and connect with friends.** [Game Center](https://developer.apple.com/game-center/) functions as Apple's social gaming network, accessible on all platforms. Game Center enables users to monitor their progress and achievements while allowing developers to implement leaderboards, challenges, and multiplayer features within the game. For design guidance, refer to [Game Center](game-center.md); for developer instructions, see [GameKit](apple:GameKit).
- **Allow players to resume their game on any device.** Many users utilize a single iCloud account across different Apple devices. By supporting [GameSave](apple:GameSave), you facilitate saving the game state, allowing players to restart precisely where they stopped on a different device.
- **Support haptics to enhance the player's feeling of action.** Implementing Core Haptics allows you to compose and trigger unique haptic patterns, which can optionally be paired with custom audio. Core Haptics is supported across iOS, iPadOS, tvOS, and visionOS, and many game controllers are compatible. For implementation guidance, consult [Playing haptics](playing-haptics.md); for developer instructions, see [Core Haptics](apple:CoreHaptics) and [Playing Haptics on Game Controllers](apple:CoreHaptics/playing-haptics-on-game-controllers).
- **Utilize Spatial Audio to fully immerse players in your game's soundscape.** Offering multichannel audio allows your game's sound to automatically adjust based on the current device, facilitating an immersive Spatial Audio experience where hardware supports it. For guidance, see [visionOS](playing-audio.md#visionOS); for developer instructions, see [Explore Spatial Audio](https://developer.apple.com/news/?id=fakg1z5b).
- **Leverage Apple technologies to enable unique gameplay mechanics.** For instance, you may integrate technologies such as augmented reality, machine learning, and [HealthKit](apple:HealthKit), or request access to location data alongside functionalities like the camera and microphone. For a comprehensive list of Apple technologies, features, and services, refer to [Technologies](technologies.md).
