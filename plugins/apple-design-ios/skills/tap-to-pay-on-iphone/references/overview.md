# Tap to Pay on iPhone — overview
> **Note**
> Tap to Pay on iPhone functions in conjunction with your current payment acceptance hardware and peripherals.

To integrate Tap to Pay on iPhone into your iOS application, you must collaborate with a supported payment service provider (PSP), obtain the Tap to Pay on iPhone entitlement, and utilize the [ProximityReader](apple:ProximityReader) APIs. This can be done either via the PSP’s SDK or by adopting the framework directly. For comprehensive guidance—including marketing advice on announcing your app's new features—refer to [Tap to Pay on iPhone](https://developer.apple.com/tap-to-pay/). For developer instructions, consult [Setting up Tap to Pay on iPhone](apple:ProximityReader/setting-up-the-entitlement-for-tap-to-pay-on-iPhone).

> **Note**
> If your PSP provides an SDK that includes user interfaces for scenarios such as displaying a tap result, consult the documentation supplied by your PSP.
