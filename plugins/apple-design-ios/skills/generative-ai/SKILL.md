# Generative AI
Generative AI enables you to enhance your application or game by incorporating dynamic content and intelligent features, thereby unlocking advanced levels of creativity, connectivity, and productivity

## When to use
- User asks about **generative ai** on iOS and iPadOS (e.g. `"how do I design generative ai for iPhone"`).
- User is building an iPhone UI that needs generative ai and wants to follow Apple's guidelines.
- User asks to audit or review generative ai in an iOS and iPadOS design.
- User mentions generative ai in the context of an iPhone app, game, or interface.

## Quick principles
- **Design your experience responsibly** — Responsible AI involves the deliberate design and development of AI features, taking into account their direct and indirect effects on individuals, systems…
- **Keep people in control** — Although AI can generate and manipulate content, you must respect users' agency and ensure they maintain command over decision-making and the overall…
- **Ensure an inclusive experience for all** — AI models learn from data and naturally tend to favor the most prevalent information
- **Design engaging and useful generative features** — Generative AI is a potent tool, but it is not suitable for every scenario
- **Ensure a great experience even when generative features aren’t available or people opt not to use them** — In some instances, generative AI may be integral to the experience, leaving no practical non-AI substitute
- **Communicate where your app uses AI** — Inform users about when and how your application utilizes artificial intelligence
- **Set clear expectations about what your AI-powered feature can and can’t do** — Defining the capabilities and constraints of your experience assists users in forming an accurate mental model of the feature
- **Prefer on-device processing** — Depending on your application requirements, you may achieve excellent results using models run locally on the device, which ensures user information never…
- **Ask permission before using personal information and usage data** — Certain interactions with an AI model may involve sensitive inputs, such as private messages, images, or feature usage statistics
- **Clearly disclose how your app and its model use and store personal information** — Users are more inclined to share data when they fully comprehend how it will be utilized
- **Thoughtfully evaluate model capabilities** — Generative models differ; some possess general knowledge, while others are trained for specific functions
- **Be intentional when choosing or creating a dataset** — The data selected profoundly affects the model's behavior, whether you are training from scratch or customizing an existing model
