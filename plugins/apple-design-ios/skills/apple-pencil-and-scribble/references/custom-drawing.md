# Custom drawing
Using [PencilKit](apple:PencilKit), you can enable users to take notes, annotate images and documents, and draw with the same low-latency performance found in iOS. PencilKit also facilitates building a custom drawing canvas within your application and providing a sophisticated tool picker and ink palette.

- **Enable users to draw on top of existing content.** By default, the colors used on your PencilKit canvas automatically adapt to Dark Mode, allowing users to create content in either mode while ensuring excellent viewing quality. However, when markup is applied over existing material such as a PDF or photograph, you must disable the dynamic color adjustment to ensure the markup remains crisp and clearly visible.
- **Consider implementing custom undo and redo controls when your application operates in a compact environment.** While the tool picker includes undo and redo buttons in standard environments, they are absent in a compact environment. In this latter scenario, you may display undo and redo buttons within a toolbar. Additionally, consider supporting the standard three-finger gesture for undo/redo functionality so users can access it regardless of the environment. For detailed guidance, refer to [Undo and redo](undo-and-redo.md).
