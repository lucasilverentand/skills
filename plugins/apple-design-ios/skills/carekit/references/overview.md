# CareKit — overview
CareKit 2.0 comprises two distinct projects: CareKit UI and CareKit Store. CareKit UI offers numerous ready-made views that developers can utilize to construct a personalized CareKit application. Conversely, CareKit Store establishes the database schema that integrates CareKit entities—such as patients, care plans, tasks, and contacts—allowing for the storage and management of patient data on their device. CareKit 2.0 ensures fluid synchronization between your database and the UI, guaranteeing that care plans remain current at all times. Developer instructions are available at [CareKit](https://carekit-apple.github.io/CareKit/documentation/carekit).
