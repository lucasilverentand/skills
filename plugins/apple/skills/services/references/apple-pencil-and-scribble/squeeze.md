# Squeeze
When using Apple Pencil Pro, a squeeze action can be performed. You may design custom behavior for this squeeze response, but remember that users might configure the gesture to execute an **App Shortcuts** command instead of an action specific to your app.

> **Note**
> The squeeze gesture is only functional when the paired iPad screen is active and the Apple Pencil Pro is not making physical contact with it. Since squeeze operates even when there is a gap between the Apple Pencil Pro and iPad, users may not always be visually aware of the gesture's resulting on-screen action.

- **Handle squeeze as a singular, rapid gesture that executes a distinct — non-continuous — action.** Since users may apply significant force or perform multiple squeezes quickly, which can be fatiguing, ensure your response is triggered by a single squeeze and immediately displays the outcome.
- **If you use squeeze to display app UI, such as a contextual menu, present it near the Apple Pencil Pro.** Showing the result of the squeeze close to the Apple Pencil Pro tip reinforces the connection between the device and the gesture, aiding user engagement with their task.
- **Ensure squeeze actions are nondestructive and easily reversible.** Similar to the double-tap gesture, users might activate the squeeze unintentionally; therefore, it is crucial that you do not use this action to cause data loss.
