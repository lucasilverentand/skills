# Creating App Clips for businesses
If you are a platform provider offering services to businesses, you have the option of creating several App Clip experiences in [App Store Connect](https://appstoreconnect.apple.com) or utilizing one App Clip to support all of them. To the end-user, the App Clip displays the branding of a specific business or location rather than your own platform's branding.

- **Maintain consistent branding.** When users view the App Clip card for a business, that business's brand must be prominently featured. Minimize your own branding and ensure the business's identity is clearly visible to prevent confusion upon entering the App Clip experience.
- **Anticipate multiple businesses.** An App Clip might support numerous different businesses or a single business with different locations. In either scenario, users may utilize the App Clip for more than one business or location concurrently. The App Clip must accommodate this scenario and adjust its user interface accordingly. For instance, consider implementing a mechanism to switch between recent businesses or locations and validating the user's location upon launch.

For guidance intended for developers, consult [Configuring App Clip experiences](apple:AppClip/configuring-the-launch-experience-of-your-app-clip).
