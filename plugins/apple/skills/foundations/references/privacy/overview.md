# Privacy — overview
Upon submitting a new or revised application, you must furnish specifics regarding your data collection practices and the privacy-relevant information gathered. This enables the App Store to present this information on your product page. (You retain the ability to manage these details at any time within [App Store Connect](https://help.apple.com/app-store-connect/#/dev1b4647c5b).) Potential users rely on these privacy disclosures to make an informed decision before downloading your app. For further details, consult [App privacy details on the App Store](https://developer.apple.com/app-store/app-privacy-details/).
