# Platform guidance — watchOS
- **Employ background color to either reinforce current content or convey supplementary details.** Background hue can establish context and assist users in identifying crucial information. For instance, within Activity, the infographic view for the Move, Exercise, and Stand rings utilizes a background color matching that of the ring itself. Utilize background color when there is a message to transmit, rather than merely serving as visual decoration. Refrain from using full-screen background color in views users may view for extended durations, such as during a workout or while an audio application is running.
- **Acknowledge that users may prefer graphic complications over full color when using tinted mode.** The system has the capability to employ a singular color derived from the wearer's chosen hue within a graphic complication's images, gauges, and text. Refer to **Complications** for further guidance.
