# Getting started
Develop an application or game that delivers a cohesive, native experience across all supported platforms

## Topics
- **Designing for iOS** — Users rely on their iPhone as a constant companion for staying connected, playing games, consuming media, completing tasks, and tracking personal data anywhere.
- **Designing for iPadOS** — Users value the combination of power, mobility, and adaptability of the iPad for enjoying media, playing games, and undertaking detailed productivity or creative projects.
- **Designing for macOS** — Users depend on the Mac's power, expansive canvas, and flexibility when performing intensive productivity tasks, viewing content, or gaming, often utilizing multiple applications simultaneously.
- **Designing for tvOS** — Users appreciate the vivid content, immersive experiences, and simplified interactions that tvOS provides across media, gaming, fitness, education, and home companion applications.
- **Designing for visionOS** — When users wear Apple Vision Pro, they enter an infinite 3D environment where your application or game can be experienced while remaining aware of the physical surroundings.
- **Designing for watchOS** — When users check their Apple Watch, they know it provides immediate access to vital information and allows them to complete simple, time-sensitive tasks regardless of whether they are moving or still.
- **Designing for games** — When users play your game on an Apple device, they immerse themselves in the world you created while benefiting from the platform's inherent features.
