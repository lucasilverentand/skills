# Foundations

## Topics
- **Accessibility** — Accessible user interfaces enable everyone to have a positive experience with your application or game.
- **App icons** — A unique, memorable icon conveys the app’s or game's purpose and personality, allowing users to recognize it instantly.
- [Branding](branding.md) — Apps and games express their distinct brand identity, making them instantly recognizable while feeling native to the platform and ensuring a consistent experience.
- **Color** — Thoughtful use of color can enhance communication, evoke your brand, provide visual continuity, communicate status and feedback, and aid users in understanding information.
- **Dark Mode** — Dark Mode is a system appearance setting that uses a dark color palette, offering comfortable viewing in low-light environments.
- **Icons** — An effective icon is a graphic asset that conveys a single concept in a manner users immediately grasp.
- **Images** — To ensure your artwork renders perfectly on all supported devices, learn how the system displays content and deliver art using appropriate scale factors.
- **Immersive experiences** — In visionOS, you can design apps and games that extend beyond windows and volumes, fully immersing users in your content.
- **Inclusion** — Inclusive apps and games prioritize the user by maintaining respectful communication and presenting content and functionality in ways that everyone can access and comprehend.
- **Layout** — A consistent layout that adjusts to different contexts makes your experience more approachable and helps users enjoy their favorite apps and games on all devices.
- **Materials** — A material is a visual effect that creates a sense of depth, layering, and hierarchy between foreground and background elements.
- **Motion** — Beautiful, fluid motions bring the interface to life by conveying status, providing feedback and instructions, and enriching the visual experience of your app or game.
- **Privacy** — Privacy is paramount: it is critical to be transparent regarding the privacy-related data and resources you require, and equally essential is protecting the data users grant access to.
- **Right to left** — Support right-to-left languages like Arabic and Hebrew by reversing your interface as needed to match the reading direction of the related scripts.
- **SF Symbols** — SF Symbols provides thousands of consistent, highly configurable symbols that integrate seamlessly with the San Francisco system font, automatically aligning with text in all weights and sizes.
- **Spatial layout** — Spatial layout techniques help you take advantage of the infinite canvas of Apple Vision Pro and present your content in engaging, comfortable ways.
- **Typography** — Your typographic choices can help you display legible text, convey an information hierarchy, communicate important content, and express your brand or style.
- **Writing** — The words you choose within your app are a vital component of its overall user experience.
