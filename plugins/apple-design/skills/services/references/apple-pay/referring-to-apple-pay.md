# Referring to Apple Pay
When referring to Apple Pay, use the exact phrasing found in [Apple Trademark List](https://www.apple.com/legal/intellectual-property/trademark/appletmlist.html) — never pluralize or make it possessive — and comply with [Guidelines for Using Apple Trademarks](https://www.apple.com/legal/intellectual-property/guidelinesfor3rdparties.html).

You may use plain text to advertise Apple Pay and denote that it is a payment method.

**Capitalize Apple Pay in text exactly as shown in the Apple Trademark list.** Use two words, with an uppercase *A*, an uppercase *P*, and all other letters in lowercase. Display Apple Pay entirely in capital letters only when required to match an established, typographic interface style (e.g., within an app that capitalizes all text).

**Do not use the Apple logo to represent the word *Apple* in body text.** In the United States, include the registered trademark symbol (®) upon the initial appearance of Apple Pay in body text. Do not include a registered trademark symbol when Apple Pay appears as a selection option during checkout.

||Example text|
|---|---|
|✓|Purchase with Apple Pay|
|✓|Purchase with Apple Pay®|
|✗|Purchase with ApplePay|
|✗|Purchase with  Pay|
|✗|Purchase with APPLE PAY|

**Match the font face and size to your application.** Do not attempt to replicate Apple's typography. Instead, utilize text attributes that remain consistent with the rest of your website or app.

**Do not translate *Apple Pay* or any other Apple trademark.** Always present Apple trademarks in English, even if they appear within non-English content.

- **In a payment selection scenario, you may display a text-only description of Apple Pay only if all other payment options also have text-only descriptions.** If any alternative payment option description includes an icon or logo, you must use the Apple Pay mark as detailed in [Offering Apple Pay](#Offering-Apple-Pay).
- **When promoting your app's integration of Apple Pay, follow App Store guidelines.** Before marketing Apple Pay for your application, consult the [App Store marketing guidelines](https://developer.apple.com/app-store/marketing/guidelines/).
