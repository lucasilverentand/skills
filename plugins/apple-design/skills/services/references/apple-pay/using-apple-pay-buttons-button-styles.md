# Button styles
The `automatic` style allows the current system appearance to dictate how Apple Pay buttons are rendered in your application. (Refer to [PKPaymentButtonStyle.automatic](apple:PassKit/PKPaymentButtonStyle/automatic) for developer guidance.) If you wish to manually control the button's appearance, you may utilize one of the following options. Web developers should consult [ApplePayButtonStyle](apple:ApplePayontheWeb/ApplePayButtonStyle) for relevant guidance.

##### Black
Apply this element against white or light backgrounds that offer adequate contrast. Do not use it on black or dark themes.

##### White with outline
Apply this against white or light backgrounds when adequate contrast is unavailable. Avoid placing it on dark or highly saturated colors.

##### White
Apply this on dark backgrounds that offer adequate contrast.
