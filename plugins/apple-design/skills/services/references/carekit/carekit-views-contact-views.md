# Contact views
A care plan usually involves a dedicated team and trusted individuals who assist patients in adhering to the plan. CareKit UI provides a contact view that facilitates patient communication with these care plan members.

In CareKit 2.0, the CareKit UI offers two styles for this contact view: simple and detailed.

**Consider using color to categorize care team members.** Color serves as a visual aid, allowing users to identify specific team roles at a glance.
