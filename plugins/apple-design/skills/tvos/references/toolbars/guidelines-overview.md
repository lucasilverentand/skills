# Toolbars — full guidelines — overview
Toolbars perform actions on content within the view, facilitate movement through the app, and help users maintain orientation. They incorporate three types of content:

- The title identifying the current view
- Navigation controls, such as back and forward functions, and **Search fields**
- Actions (or bar items), including **Buttons** and **Menus**

In contrast to a toolbar, **Tab bars** are specifically designed for navigating between distinct areas of an application.
