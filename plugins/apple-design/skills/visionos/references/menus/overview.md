# Menus — overview
> **Note**
> Certain system components also incorporate menus tailored for specific scenarios. For example, a **Pop-up buttons** or **Pull-down buttons** may present a menu of options directly tied to their action; a **Context menus** allows users quick access to a limited set of actions relevant to their current view or task; and in macOS and iPadOS, **The menu bar** menus house all commands available within the app or game.
