# Suggesting problem resolutions
- **Allow users to reset the experience if it falls short of expectations.** Do not compel users to wait for conditions to improve or struggle with object placement. Provide a way for them to restart and achieve better results.
- **Offer potential fixes when problems occur.** Analysis of the real-world environment or surface detection may fail or require excessive time due to different factors—insufficient light, an overly reflective surface, lack of surface detail, or excessive camera movement. If your app is informed of these issues, use clear and friendly language to suggest ways to resolve them.

|Problem|Possible suggestion|
|---|---|
|Insufficient features detected.|Try increasing the lighting and moving around.|
|Excessive motion detected.|Attempt to move your phone more slowly.|
|Surface detection takes too long.|Try moving around, increasing the lights, and ensuring your phone is aimed at a sufficiently textured surface.|
