# Top Shelf — full guidelines — overview
Top Shelf presents a unique chance to highlight new, featured, or recommended material and enable users to jump directly into your app or game. For example, selecting Apple TV in the Dock immediately initiates full-screen previews, after which the Dock minimizes. While viewing the initial preview, users can swipe through other featured show previews or select Play or More Info for any content that catches their interest.

The system provides several layout templates you can utilize to deliver a compelling Top Shelf experience when your app is selected in the Dock. To assist with content positioning, these templates are available for download from [Apple Design Resources](https://developer.apple.com/design/resources/#tvos-apps).
