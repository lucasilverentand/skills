# Text fields
A text field is a rectangular region where users input or modify brief, specific textual data

## Platform guidance — iOS & iPadOS
- **Include a Clear button at the trailing edge of a text field to assist users in removing their input.** When this element is present, tapping it allows the user to wipe the text field's contents without needing continuous use of the Delete key.
- **Employ images and buttons to enhance the clarity and functionality of text fields.** You may display custom images at either end of a text field, or you can incorporate a system-provided button, such as the Bookmarks feature. Generally, designate the leading end of a text field to convey its purpose and the trailing end for supplementary functions like bookmarking.
