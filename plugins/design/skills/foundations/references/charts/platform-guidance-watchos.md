# Platform guidance — watchOS
Generally, minimize complex chart interactions within your watchOS application. Focus on presenting information that users can grasp instantly and support only simple interactions when they provide value. If you offer a version of your app on another platform, consider using that platform to display greater detail and support advanced chart interactions. For example, the watchOS Heart Rate feature displays the wearer’s heart-rate data for the current day, whereas the iPhone Health app presents heart-rate data across multiple time periods and allows users to examine individual readings.
