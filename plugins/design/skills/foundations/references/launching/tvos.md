# Launching
A smooth launch experience enables users to begin using your app or game immediately

## Platform guidance — tvOS
> **Note**
Unlike most tvOS applications that utilize **Layered images**, the launch screen remains static.

**In a live-viewing application, consider automatically initiating playback shortly after the app is launched.** Users access your application specifically to watch television; therefore, it may be beneficial to begin playing new or previously viewed live content after a short period of inactivity. For more information, consult **Live-viewing apps**.
