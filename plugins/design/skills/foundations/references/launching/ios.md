# Launching
A smooth launch experience enables users to begin using your app or game immediately

## Platform guidance — iOS & iPadOS
**Launch in the correct orientation.** If your application or game supports both portrait and landscape modes, launch using whatever orientation the device is currently in. If your interface operates exclusively within one orientation, initialize it in that mode and permit users to rotate the device if required. Furthermore, ensure that a landscape-only interface behaves correctly, regardless of whether users enter the landscape orientation by rotating from either the left or right. For further information, consult **Layout**.
