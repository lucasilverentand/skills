# Technologies
Explore the Apple technologies, features, and services available for integration into your application or game

## Topics
- **AirPlay** — enables wireless media streaming from iOS, iPadOS, macOS, and tvOS devices to compatible displays like Apple TV, HomePod, or supported TVs/speakers.
- **Always On** — allows the system to keep an app's interface visible on devices with the Always On display, even when user interaction pauses.
- **App Clips** — provides a lightweight, instantly accessible version of your app or game for on-the-go use or demonstrations.
- **Apple Pay** — offers a secure and simple method for payments—including donations and subscriptions, as well as physical goods and services—across apps (iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Vision Pro, Apple Watch), websites, and browsers.
- **Augmented reality** — allows developers to deliver immersive, engaging experiences by seamlessly merging virtual objects with the physical environment.
- **CareKit** — enables apps to help users manage care plans for chronic conditions (e.g., diabetes), aid recovery from injury or surgery, or assist in achieving health and wellness objectives.
- **CarPlay** — allows users to navigate, make calls, send/receive messages, stream music, and perform other functions using the car's display while maintaining focus on driving.
- **Game Center** — is Apple's social gaming network, allowing players to track their progress and connect with friends across Apple platforms, thereby increasing game discovery on users' devices.
- **Generative AI** — empowers developers to enhance apps or games with dynamic content and intelligent features, unlocking new levels of creativity, connection, and productivity.
- **HealthKit** — serves as the central repository for health and fitness data across iOS, iPadOS, and watchOS.
- **HomeKit** — allows users to securely manage connected home accessories using Siri or the Home app on iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac.
- **iCloud** — is a service enabling users to access their important content—such as photos, videos, and documents—from any device without needing explicit synchronization.
- **ID Verifier** — allows your iPhone app to read mobile IDs in person without requiring additional external hardware.
- **iMessage apps and stickers** — — An iMessage app facilitates content sharing, collaboration, and gaming within a conversation; stickers are images used to enhance the visual aspect of a chat.
- **In-app purchase** — allows users to securely pay for virtual items—such as subscriptions, premium content, or digital goods—directly within your application.
- **Live Photos** — enables users to capture cherished memories through a sound- and motion-rich interactive experience, adding life to static photographs.
- **Mac Catalyst** — allows developers to create a Mac version of an iPad app, offering users the chance to enjoy the experience in a new environment.
- **Machine learning** — enables apps and games to learn from usage data and patterns, allowing for the enhancement of current experiences and the creation of novel ones.
- **Maps** — displays geographical data, whether indoor or outdoor, within your application or website.
- **NFC** — — Near-field communication allows devices within close proximity (a few centimeters) to exchange information wirelessly.
- **Photo editing** — extensions enable users to modify photos and videos inside the Photos app by applying filters or making different adjustments.
- **ResearchKit** — enables apps to allow individuals globally to participate in significant medical research studies.
- **SharePlay** — facilitates shared activities—such as watching a film, listening to music, playing a game, or brainstorming on a whiteboard—while participants are in a FaceTime call or Messages conversation.
- **ShazamKit** — supports audio recognition by comparing an audio sample against either the ShazamKit catalog or a user-defined custom catalog.
- **Sign in with Apple** — offers a quick, private method for signing into apps and websites, providing users with a trustworthy consistent experience and eliminating the need to remember multiple accounts and passwords.
- **Siri** — allows users to accomplish daily tasks efficiently using voice commands, touch input, or automation.
- **Tap to Pay on iPhone** — enables merchants to accept contactless payments using only an app on their iPhone, eliminating the need for external hardware.
- [VoiceOver](voiceover.md) — is a screen reader that allows users to interact with your app’s interface without requiring visual input.
- **Wallet** — allows users to securely store different items on iPhone and Apple Watch, including credit/debit cards, driver’s licenses or state IDs, transit passes, event tickets, and keys.
