# System experiences

## Topics
- **App Shortcuts** — Allows users to access your application's critical functions or content across the entire system.
- **Complications** — Presents current, pertinent information on the watch face, viewable whenever the user raises their wrist.
- **Controls** — Offers rapid access to a feature within your app via Control Center, the Lock Screen, or the Action button.
- **Live Activities** — Enables users to monitor the advancement of a task, event, or activity at a glance.
- **Notifications** — Delivers timely information of high value that users can grasp immediately.
- [Status bars](status-bars.md) — Appears along the top edge of the screen, displaying information regarding the device's current condition, such as battery level, time, or cellular carrier.
- **Top Shelf** — This area on the Apple TV Home Screen showcases your content in a rich, engaging manner while also granting users access to their preferred apps within the Dock.
- **Watch faces** — Represents the primary view that users select within watchOS.
- **Widgets** — Offers swift access to vital information and focused interactions from your application or game within different contexts.
