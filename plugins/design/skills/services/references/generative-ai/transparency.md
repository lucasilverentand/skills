# Transparency
- **Communicate where your app uses AI.** Inform users about when and how your application utilizes artificial intelligence. This manages expectations and allows individuals to make an informed decision regarding the use of AI-driven features. Crucially, never mislead a user into believing they are interacting with or viewing content created by a human if the interaction is actually powered by AI. Ensure your disclosure method complies with all applicable regional regulations where you distribute your app.
- **Set clear expectations about what your AI-powered feature can and can’t do.** Defining the capabilities and constraints of your experience assists users in forming an accurate mental model of the feature. For instance, when introducing a new capability, consider providing a short tutorial. If your feature is open-ended (such as a generation prompt or search bar), consider offering curated suggestions to facilitate initial use. If the feature has known limitations, inform users about them proactively, demonstrate how to achieve optimal outcomes, and explain the cause when suboptimal results appear. For further guidance, refer to **Limitations**.
