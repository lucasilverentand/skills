# Privacy
- **Prefer on-device processing.** Depending on your application requirements, you may achieve excellent results using models run locally on the device, which ensures user information never leaves the hardware. For instance, you might utilize the on-device models provided by the Apple Foundation Models framework. Furthermore, these local models can respond faster than server-based counterparts and remain functional even without an internet connection. Server-based models are typically appropriate when the task requires larger, more powerful models demanding greater memory and processing capacity than a typical device possesses. Always weigh the privacy implications against the user experience when selecting a model type. If server-based processing is employed, process as much data locally as possible and minimize what is transmitted. Users must be informed if their information may go to a server, what data is being shared, and whether any data will be stored off-device or utilized for training.
- **Ask permission before using personal information and usage data.** Certain interactions with an AI model may involve sensitive inputs, such as private messages, images, or feature usage statistics. After gaining consent, utilize only the minimum amount of data required and consistently provide a clear mechanism for users to decline its use. If sensitive data is needed for model enhancement or storage, explicit consent must be obtained and handled with due care. If data is shared with third parties, ensure you understand their privacy protocols. Remember that model outputs may unintentionally contain sensitive information. Note that [apps for kids](https://developer.apple.com/app-store/kids-apps/) are subject to stricter regulations regarding data usage. For guidance, consult [Requesting permission](privacy.md#Requesting-permission).
- **Clearly disclose how your app and its model use and store personal information.** Users are more inclined to share data when they fully comprehend how it will be utilized. Give users the power to make an informed decision regarding what data they allow your AI model to access. When requesting permission for information usage, explain the benefits in a manner that is precise, easily digestible, and concise. Specify whether your model incorporates personal information into its training or improvement processes.
