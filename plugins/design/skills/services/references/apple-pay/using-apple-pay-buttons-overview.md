# Using Apple Pay buttons — overview
The system furnishes multiple Apple Pay button types and visual styles available for integration into your application or website. Conversely, the [Apple Pay mark](#Apple-Pay-mark) should be utilized solely to signify that Apple Pay is an available payment method.

You must not create proprietary designs for your Apple Pay buttons, nor should you attempt to imitate the button styles provided by the system.

For detailed developer instructions, refer to [PKPaymentButtonType](apple:PassKit/PKPaymentButtonType) and [PKPaymentButtonStyle](apple:PassKit/PKPaymentButtonStyle) (for iOS and macOS), [WKInterfacePaymentButton](apple:WatchKit/WKInterfacePaymentButton) (for watchOS), and [Apple Pay on the Web](apple:ApplePayontheWeb) (for web implementations).
