# Live Activities — overview
For instance, a Live Activity could display the estimated arrival time for a food delivery, live data from an ongoing soccer match, or real-time fitness metrics and controls to pause or terminate a workout.

Live Activities initiate on iPhone or iPad and automatically appear in system locations across a user's devices:

|Platform or system experience|Location|
|---|---|
|iPhone and iPad|Lock Screen, Home Screen, in the Dynamic Island and StandBy on iPhone|
|Mac|The menu bar|
|Apple Watch|Smart Stack|
|CarPlay|CarPlay Dashboard|
