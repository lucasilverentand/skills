# Displaying buttons — overview
Apple offers several Sign in with Apple buttons that users can utilize for account creation and sign-in. If you need to create a custom button, consult [Creating a custom Sign in with Apple button](#Creating-a-custom-Sign-in-with-Apple-button) for the necessary guidelines.

**Display the Sign in with Apple button prominently.** Ensure its size is equal to or greater than other sign-in buttons, and prevent users from needing to scroll to locate it.
