# Making signup effortless
A straightforward and informative sign-up experience allows users to easily follow through on their interest in your content, regardless of whether they are within your application or viewing your App Store product page.

- **Provide clear, distinguishable subscription options.** Use brief, self-explanatory titles that differentiate the plans, and specify the price and term for each option. If an introductory rate is offered, ensure you list the initial price, the offer's duration, and the standard pricing that applies once the promotion concludes.
- **Simplify initial signup by asking only for necessary information.** A lengthy enrollment process can decrease your subscription conversion rate. Postpone requesting additional details until after the user has successfully signed up.
- **In your tvOS app, help people sign up or authenticate using another device.** Rather than requiring users to input information directly into your tvOS app, send a code to a separate device where they can enter the required details.
- **Give people more information in your app’s sign-up screen.** In addition to including links to your Terms of Service and Privacy Policy in both the app and App Store metadata, the in-app sign-up screen must include:
- The subscription name, its duration, and the content or services included during each billing cycle.
- The billing amount, accurately localized for all territories and currencies where the subscription is available for purchase.
- A mechanism allowing current subscribers to sign in or restore purchases.

For instance, the Forest Explorer sign-up screen prominently displays billing totals for monthly, biannual, and annual subscriptions. Secondary placement is used to show the price breakdown for biannual and annual plans, enabling users to compare values and make an informed decision. The screen also features a button for existing subscribers to restore their purchases.

- **Clearly describe how a free trial works.** It is crucial that users understand that payment will automatically begin for the subsequent subscription period once the free trial expires. For example, the Ocean Journal sign-up screen explicitly details both the free trial length and the amount that will be charged upon its conclusion.
- **Include a sign-up opportunity in your app’s settings.** Account and application settings are common locations where users look for subscription options.
