# Barrel roll
When marking with Apple Pencil Pro, users can employ a barrel-roll gesture to change the nature of their mark. For example, if you are using Apple Pencil Pro in Notes to highlight content, a barrel roll will rotate the angle of that mark.

**Use barrel roll only to modify marking behavior, not to enable navigation or display other controls.** Unlike double tap or squeeze, the barrel roll is intrinsically linked to the act of marking and should not be used for performing interface actions.
