# Game Center — overview
- Find new games that their friends are playing.
- Effortlessly invite companions to play.
- View recent activity from their games across the entire system, including within the Apple Games app, the App Store, and notifications.

By enabling these player activities, supporting Game Center also increases your game's reach among users across Apple platforms.

You can integrate Game Center using the GameKit framework. This framework offers a complete UI that allows players to easily access and view their Game Center information within your game. Alternatively, you may utilize GameKit to display this data using your own custom user interface. For developer instructions, refer to [GameKit](apple:GameKit).
