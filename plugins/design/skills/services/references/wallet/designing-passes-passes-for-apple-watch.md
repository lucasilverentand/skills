# Passes for Apple Watch
On Apple Watch, Wallet presents passes within a scrolling carousel format. Since users can add your pass to their Apple Watch even without developing a watch-specific application, it is crucial to understand how your pass will appear on the device.

Users can tap a pass on their Apple Watch to bring up a details screen, which presents additional data in a scrollable view. Additionally, depending on the pass, users may be able to tap a specific transaction for further details.
