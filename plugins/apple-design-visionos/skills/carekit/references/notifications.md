# Notifications
Notifications can alert users when medication is due or a task needs completion, and badging your application icon can signal an unread message from a caregiver. Your app's notifications can also be displayed on Apple Watch; consult [Notifications](notifications.md) for guidance.

- **Minimize notifications.** Care plans vary significantly among patients. While some individuals may have only a few daily tasks, others might have extensive lists. Use notifications sparingly to prevent users from feeling overwhelmed. Whenever feasible, group multiple items into a single notification.
- **Consider providing a detail view.** In addition to supplying more information, a notification detail view allows users to take immediate action without needing to open your app and leave their current context. For example, you could utilize a notification detail view to display pending tasks, enabling users to quickly mark them as complete.
