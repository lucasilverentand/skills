# SF Symbols — overview
The availability of specific symbols and features depends on the target system version. Symbols and capabilities introduced in a particular year will not be present in earlier operating systems.

Visit [SF Symbols](https://developer.apple.com/sf-symbols/) to download the application and explore the complete collection of symbols. Ensure you understand the terms and conditions for using SF Symbols, particularly the restriction against employing these symbols—or images that are similarly confusing—in app icons, logos, or any other trademarked capacity. For developer guidance, consult [Configuring and displaying symbol images in your UI](apple:UIKit/configuring-and-displaying-symbol-images-in-your-ui).
