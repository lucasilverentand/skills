# Menus — overview
> **Note**
> Certain system components also incorporate menus tailored for specific scenarios. For example, a [Pop-up buttons](pop-up-buttons.md) or [Pull-down buttons](pull-down-buttons.md) may present a menu of options directly tied to their action; a [Context menus](context-menus.md) allows users quick access to a limited set of actions relevant to their current view or task; and in macOS and iPadOS, [The menu bar](the-menu-bar.md) menus house all commands available within the app or game.
