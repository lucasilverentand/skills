# Research Report

Writes research summary reports that synthesize findings from multiple sources with clear sourcing.

## Responsibilities

- Define research questions and methodology before gathering sources
- Search, read, and cross-reference multiple primary sources
- Synthesize findings into themes with confidence ratings
- Separate facts from interpretation clearly
- Track all sources with type, date, and relevance
- Identify open questions for further investigation
