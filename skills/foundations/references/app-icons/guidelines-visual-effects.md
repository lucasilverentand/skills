# Visual effects
- **Let the system handle blurring and other visual effects.** The operating system applies dynamic visual treatments to your app icon layers, rendering it unnecessary to include elements like blurs, glows, beveled edges, specular highlights, or drop shadows between layers. Furthermore, unlike the dynamic effects provided by the system, custom effects are static and may interfere with system rendering. Should you choose to include custom visual treatments on your icon layers, apply them deliberately and rigorously test them using Icon Composer, the Simulator, or a physical device to ensure they render as intended and do not conflict with system effects.
- **Create layer groupings to apply effects to multiple layers at once.** While system effects usually target individual layers, you may group several layers within Icon Composer or your design software if applying an effect at the collective group level makes sense for your design.
