# Apple Pay mark
Use the Apple Pay mark graphic to indicate that Apple Pay is an available payment choice when displaying other payment options in a comparable manner. The Apple Pay mark itself is not a button; if you require an interactive Apple Pay button, select one of the options detailed in [Button types](#Button-types). For design guidance concerning how to present Apple Pay as a payment option, refer to [Offering Apple Pay](#Offering-Apple-Pay).

- **Only utilize the artwork provided by Apple, making no modifications other than scaling its height.** While you may define a specific height for the Apple Pay mark, ensure that this chosen dimension is equal to or greater than the heights of other payment brand marks within your transaction flow. Do not modify the width, corner radius, or aspect ratio of the artwork; refrain from adding a trademark symbol or any other content; do not remove the border; and do not apply visual effects to the mark, such as glows, reflections, or shadows. Furthermore, do not flip, rotate, or animate the Apple Pay mark.
- **Maintain a minimum clear space of 1/10th of its height around the mark.** Do not allow the Apple Pay mark to share its boundary with any other graphic or button.

Download the Apple Pay mark graphic and complete usage guidelines [here](https://developer.apple.com/apple-pay/marketing/).
