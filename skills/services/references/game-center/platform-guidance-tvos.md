# Platform guidance — tvOS
**Display an optional image at the top of the dashboard.** This feature allows you to add supplementary artwork to the tvOS dashboard, enhancing your game's aesthetic. The image should be simple and easily recognizable, maintaining visual quality when viewed from a distance. While utilizing your game's logo or word mark is recommended, do not use the app icon for this image. Use the following specifications when creating your dashboard image:

|Attribute|Value|
|---|---|
|Image size|600x180 pt (1200x360 px @2x)|
|Format|PNG, TIF, or JPG|
|Color space|sRGB or P3|
|Resolution|72 DPI (minimum)|
