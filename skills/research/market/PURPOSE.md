# Market Research

Market sizing, user research synthesis, trend analysis, and pricing research.

## Responsibilities

- Estimate market size and opportunity
- Synthesize user research findings
- Analyze trends and pricing models
- Segment target audiences and define personas
- Evaluate go-to-market channels and distribution strategies
- Monitor industry reports and benchmark data

## Tools

- `tools/persona-builder.ts` — generate user personas from survey data and research notes
- `tools/pricing-model.ts` — simulate revenue projections across different pricing tiers and conversion rates
- `tools/trend-digest.ts` — aggregate and summarize recent industry trends from curated RSS feeds and sources
